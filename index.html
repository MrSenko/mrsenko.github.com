How it works

Every time you use open source libraries or packages to build your products there is a certain level of risk involved. Sometimes the library doesn't cover all the functionality you need, sometimes the upstream developer is not responsive or simply doesn't have the time and resources to deal with issues that are important to you. In these situations developers, who are on a tight schedule to build the next revolutionary product are faced with a challenge: take time to improve the open source library or work around the problems and bear the risks of doing so.

Mr. Senko is here to help you! Our job is to test and improve open source packages, libraries and frameworks used in developing your applications and products. We live and breathe open source and will be your trusted upstream liason. Mr. Senko will provide independently built packages which you can use in your development process. In the mean time we will contribute all the tests and bug fixes to the upstream open source community for inclusion in their next version.

How to get a bug fixed?

  1. Report the problem to us;
  2. Mr. Senko will reproduce, test and fix the problem;
  3. Download and use the latest version released by Mr. Senko!

Our technical support is available via subscription. Every subscription includes unlimited access to all currently supported software and unlimited number of support requests!
